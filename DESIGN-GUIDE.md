## Color Palette

- **Primary (Rose Pink)**: `#E63973` - Used for primary CTAs, active states, and branding.
- **Secondary (Boutique Cream)**: `#FFF5F7` - Primary background for warmth and luxury.
- **Accent (Emerald)**: `#10B981` - Success states and trust badges.
- **Text (Slate)**: `#0F172A` - High contrast for premium legibility.

## Typography

- **Headings**: `Playfair Display` (Serif) - Weights: 700, 900.
- **Body**: `Poppins` (Sans-serif) - Weights: 400, 500, 600.
- **Tracking**: Tight for headings, wide (`0.1em`) for uppercase labels.

## Components Style

- **Border Radius**: `3rem` for large sections, `1.5rem` for cards/buttons.
- **Shadows**: Soft, multi-layered (`0 20px 50px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`).
- **Transitions**: `500ms cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` for smooth reveals.
